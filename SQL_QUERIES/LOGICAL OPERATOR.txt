*********************************************************************** LOGICAL OPERATORS  ****************************************************************

NOTE: LOGICAL OPERATORS (AND, OR, NOT)
=====	1)AND OPERATOR: Returns TRUE if all the conditions are satisfied.
	2)OR OPERATOR: Returns TRUE if any one of the conditions is satisfied.
	3)NOT OPERATOR: It is an inverse operator. The selected value will be rejected.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Q1) WAQTD DETAILS OF THE EMPLOYEES WORKING AS CLERK AND EARNING LESS THAN 1500.
--->
SELECT *
FROM EMP
WHERE JOB=’CLERK’ AND SAL<1500;


Q2) WAQTD NAME AND HIREDATE OF THE EMPLOYEES WORKING AS MANAGER IN DEPT 30.
--->
SELECT ENAME, HIREDATE 
FROM EMP
WHERE JOB=’MANAGER’ AND DEPTNO=30;


Q3) WAQTD DETAILS OF THE EMPLOYEES ALONG WITH ANNUAL SALARY IF THEY ARE WORKING IN DEPT 30 AS SALESMAN AND THEIR ANNUAL SALARY HAS TO BE GREATER THAN 14000.
--->
SELECT EMP.*, SAL*12 AS ANNUALSAL
FROM EMP
WHERE DEPTNO=30 AND JOB=’SALESMAN’ AND SAL*12>14000;

Q4) WAQTD ALL THE DETAILS OF THE EMPLOYEES WORKING IN DEPT 30 OR AS ANALYST.

Q6) WAQTD NAME AND SAL, ANNUAL SAL AND DEPTNO IF DEPTNO IS 20 EARNING MORE THAN 1100 AND ANNUAL SALARY EXCEEDS 12000.
