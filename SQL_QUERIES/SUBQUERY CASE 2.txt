
******************************************************* SUBQUERY CASE 2 ***********************************************************************************

Whenever we have the data to be selected and the condition to be executed or present in the different table then we use SUBQUERY.

******************************************** ASSIGNMENT ON SUBQUERY CASE 2 ********************************************************************************

Q21) WAQTD DNAME OF THE EMPLOYEES WHOSE NAME IS SMITH.
--->
SELECT DNAME
FROM DEPT
WHERE DEPTNO = (SELECT DEPTNO
                FROM EMP
                WHERE ENAME='SMITH');

Q22) WAQTD DNAME AND LOC OF THE EMPLOYEE WHOSE ENAME IS KING.
--->
SELECT DNAME, LOC
FROM DEPT
WHERE DEPTNO = (SELECT DEPTNO
                FROM EMP
                WHERE ENAME='KING');

Q23) WAQTD LOC OF THE EMP WHOSE EMPLOYEE NUMBER IS 7902.
--->
SELECT LOC
FROM DEPT
WHERE DEPTNO = (SELECT DEPTNO
                FROM EMP
                WHERE EMPNO=7902);

Q24) WAQTD DNAME AND LOC ALONG WITH DEPTNO OF THE EMPLOYEES WHOSE NAME ENDS WITH 'R'.
--->
SELECT DNAME, LOC, DEPTNO
FROM DEPT
WHERE DEPTNO IN (SELECT DEPTNO
                 FROM EMP
		 WHERE ENAME LIKE '%R');

Q25) WAQTD DNAME OF THE EMPLOYEE WHOSE DESIGNATION IS PRESIDENT.
--->
SELECT DNAME
FROM DEPT
WHERE DEPTNO IN (SELECT DEPTNO
		 FROM EMP
                 WHERE JOB='PRESIDENT');

Q27) WAQTD ENAME AND SALARIES OF THE EMPLOYEES WHO ARE WORKING IN THE SAME LOCATION CHICAGO.

Q29) WAQTD DETAILS OF THE EMP ALONG WITH ANNUAL SALARY IF EMPLOYEES ARE WORKING IN NEW YORK
